This is your opportunity at roundhouse kickin' glory. That's what. The Chuck Norrizer allows you to be the star of over 500 Chuck Norris jokes. Just don't let the big man catch you using it.

How does it work?

I see nothing gets by you, haas. Put your first and last name in the input fields and click the "GET NORRIZED" button. One Chuck Norris joke will appear, but his name will be replaced by yours.

To see another joke with your name in it, click the "GET ANOTHER JOKE" button. You can also change the name at anytime by updating the input fields, and clicking the "GET ANOTHER JOKE" button.

Is that it?

Nope. If you leave the input fields empty, and click the "GET NORRIZED" button, Chuck Norris's name will appear in all the jokes by default. I did this to increase my chances of survival when he sees this.
